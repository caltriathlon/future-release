Event Map

This map shows the layout of Bearathlon 2017 at the Berkeley marina. If you are familiar with Bearathlon, the layout is the exact same as in previous years.

The start/finish/expo area is located on the Windsurf Launch dock while transition is in shorebird park.

Please be mindful that all areas and paths between areas, with the exception of transition and the finish line, have not been roped off to the public. Please ask a volunteer to watch your stuff and do not leave things completely unattended.

Collegiate Draft Legal Sprint

The Collegiate Draft Legal Sprint triathlon combines a 750 meter swim, a 3-looped 20 kilometer bike, and a 2-looped 5 kilometer run. The entry fee will be $60.

Events

  • 7:30 AM: The women's race begins and will be the official USAT West Regional Qualifier for Collegiate Nationals!
  • 9:00 AM: The men's race begins.

Draft Legal Participants:

  • To sign up for this event, participants must be university students and members of a collegiate triathlon club.
  • Only 75 women in wave 1 (the women's division) and only 75 men in wave 2 (the men's division).
  • Only 5 men/5 women from each collegiate team will be permitted. This restriction will be lifted on August 1, 2016 if there are remaining spots.

Rules and Additional Information:

Please read through the "RULES"   tab as well as the USAT Draft Legal Rules Summary located HERE.

Keep in mind that there draft legal events tend to be dictated by different terms and it is highly advised you see the attached documents.

Collegiate Classic Sprint

The Collegiate Classic Sprint triathlon combines a 750 meter swim, a 3-looped 20 kilometer bike, and a 2-looped 5 kilometer run. The entry fee will be $60.

Events

  • 11:00 AM: The men's race begins.
  • 11:15 AM: The women's race begins.

Collegiate Classic Participants:

  • To sign up for this event, participants must be university students and members of a collegiate triathlon club.
  • Only 75 men in wave 1 (the men's division') and only 75 women in wave 2 (the women's division).
  • There will be no limit to the number of athletes that a collegiate team may bring. Registration is first-come, first-served.

Rules and Additional Information:

Please read through the "RULES"   tab.

Open Classic Sprint

The Open Classic Sprint triathlon combines a 750 meter swim, a 3-looped 20 kilometer bike, and a 2-looped 5 kilometer run. The entry fee will be $60.

Events

  • 11:30 AM: The men's race begins.
  • 11:45 AM: The women's race begins.

Open Classic Participants:

  • The Open Classic sprint is intended for community triathletes of all ages; collegiate racers are encouraged to register for the Collegiate Draft Legal or the Collegiate Classic Sprint.
  • Only 50 men in wave 1 (the men's division') and only 50 women in wave 2 (the women's division).
  • Registration is first come, first served.
